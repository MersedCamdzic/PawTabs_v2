Frequently Asked Questions
How do I create a new group?
Click the "Groups" button in the sidebar, then click the "New Group" button in the top right, enter a name, select a color, and save.
What does the wizard feature do?
The wizard automatically organizes your tabs by regrouping windows with few tabs and splitting overcrowded windows based on your settings.
How do I backup my tabs?
Backups are created automatically when using the wizard. You can also manually create backups from the Backups section in the sidebar.
